My Approach

Design Philosophy

01

Human-Centered

Every design decision starts with the user. I conduct research, build empathy through personas and testing, and iterate until the product genuinely serves the people who use it not just looks good in a mockup.

02

Detail-Obsessed

I believe the smallest details shape the entire experience. From pixel-perfect spacing to micro-interactions and accessible contrast ratios, precision is what separates good design from great design.

03

Bridge Builder

As both a designer and developer, I bridge design and engineering, translating vision into technical reality while ensuring what gets built remains faithful to intent and technically sound from the start.
